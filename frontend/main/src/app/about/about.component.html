This project was developed by Purple Team of Java night class 2022, composed by Bruno Roberto, Cristian Schauffert, Kalil Fakhouri and Mateus Felipe with the mentorship of Teacher Oliota, aiming only to demonstrate the technical knowledge acquired during the course and the presentation of the stage end to companies.

The copy of the resource is available to everyone and can be modified and changed. Its sale, distribution or transfer is prohibited. Most of the resources and technologies are open-source or evaluation testing for a determined time (Google Cloud), in order to bring the best experience of using a functional software.

Due to our objective of proving the acquired knowledge, some parts of the software are simulated due to the time and response it would take in reality, thus accelerating the delivery process, for example, so that the data processing can be seen in real time. both locally and in the cloud.
